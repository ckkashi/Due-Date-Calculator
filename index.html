Using the due date calculator

To find your due date, enter the date of the first day of your last period, and select 'show your due date'. Pregnancy normally lasts from 37 weeks to 42 weeks from the first day of your last period.

What is a Pregnancy Calculator?

A pregnancy calculator is an online tool that helps you estimate important dates in your pregnancy. You can put your last menstrual period date often called (LMP) and it will calculate and tell you your expected due date and progress of pregnancy week by week. It will also tell you the significant milestones of the journey so you can stay prepared and informed. It is indeed a very useful tool for pregnant mothers as it helps monitor their pregnancy and help them understand the development and milestones of the upcoming baby so mothers can stay informed about upcoming appointments and important ultrasounds or tests.
